Tiffany D. Do (Diễm Đỗ)

Ph.D Candidate at The University of Central Florida. Computer Science. XR/VR/HCI.

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida (UCF). My research interests are in HCI, extended reality (VR/AR), and social influence in XR. I am currently working on research that is designed to investigate how technology, specifically mixed reality, can influence the behaviors and attitudes of people. I conduct research as a member of the eXtended Reality & Training Lab (XRT) under the supervision of Professor Ryan McMahan. Previously, I received my Bachelor's and Master's in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas. I am Vietnamese-American, and I go by either Tiffany or Diễm.
